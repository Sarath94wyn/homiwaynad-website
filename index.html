Top Destinations in Wayanad

Explore the must-visit places curated just for you

Chembra Peak

Famous for the heart-shaped lake and panoramic hill views. Perfect for trekking and adventure lovers.

Banasura Sagar Dam

India's largest earth dam surrounded by misty hills — ideal for boating, sightseeing, and relaxation.

Edakkal Caves

Ancient caves with historic carvings and an exciting uphill trek. A must-visit for history and nature lovers.
